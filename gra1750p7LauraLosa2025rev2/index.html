Catastrone

About

When a planet-killing asteroid is detected on a collision course towards Earth, humanity's last hope is a nuclear strike powerful enough to fracture it. The radiation from the impact forced survivors to live underground for millennia. Generations later, the descendants of the buried emerge with advanced technology to reclaim the surface. Yet as they step into the unknown, they find a world reshaped by catastrophe… and secrets their ancestors never imagined.
